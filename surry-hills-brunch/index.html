🥑 Surry Hills Brunch

Must-try cafés for a midday–afternoon brunch · open through 12:00–15:00 · Sydney

☕ Safest bet: Bills — all-day kitchen (till 10pm), zero timing risk, and the ricotta hotcakes are the definitive Surry Hills brunch dish. Everything else below is ranked by reputation.
The real filter isn't "famous" — it's the kitchen, not the door. Surry Hills is Sydney's café heartland, so great spots are everywhere; the catch for a noon–3pm plan is that several marquee names stop cooking by 2:00–2:30pm even if the door stays open. The list below is filtered for kitchens that run through the window (early-close famous spots are in the ⚠ section at the bottom).
1Bills Surry HillsICONIC

Ricotta hotcakes with banana & honeycomb butter · sweetcorn fritters. Bill Granger's original — the globally-copied Sydney brunch.

📍 359 Crown St, Surry Hills

🕑 7:00am–10:00pm, 7 days — all-day, noon–3pm zero-risk · sit-down, mains ~A$24–32 · walk-ins fine midday

🕑 Mon–Fri 7:00am–3:30pm · Sat–Sun 7:30am–3:30pm — order by ~3:00 as the kitchen winds down · warehouse, casual, no bookings
